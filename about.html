About Equatrade

Your trusted partner in industrial equipment trading, delivering excellence across the globe.

Our Story

Equatrade International Trading FZCO was established with a clear vision: to become the bridge between global industrial equipment manufacturers and businesses across the Middle East, Asia, and Africa. Based in the dynamic business hub of Dubai, UAE, we have positioned ourselves as a premier trading company specializing in industrial solutions.

Our journey began with a commitment to quality and reliability. Over the years, we have built strong relationships with leading manufacturers and suppliers worldwide, enabling us to offer our clients access to top-tier equipment at competitive prices.

What We Do

We specialize in three core areas: Handling, Loading & Lifting Equipment Trading; Ports & Marine Guide Equipment Trade; and Oilfield & Natural Gas Equipment & Spare Parts Trading. Each division is staffed by experienced professionals who understand the unique requirements of their respective industries.

From sourcing and procurement to logistics and after-sales support, we provide end-to-end solutions that help our clients optimize their operations and achieve their business objectives.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted and reliable partner in industrial equipment trading, delivering quality products and exceptional service that exceed our clients' expectations. We are committed to building long-term relationships based on integrity, expertise, and mutual success.

Our Vision

To become the leading industrial equipment trading company in the region, recognized for our comprehensive solutions, innovative approach, and unwavering commitment to quality. We aim to expand our global footprint while maintaining the personalized service our clients value.
